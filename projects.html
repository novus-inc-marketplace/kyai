Our Projects

Explore the initiatives driving change across Kenya.

WHAT WE DO

Impactful Initiatives, Sustainable Futures

From seedling to harvest, our projects empower youth and transform communities.

Kakamega Reforestation Project

In partnership with local communities, we are working to restore the Kakamega Forest, a vital ecosystem. This project involves planting over 50,000 indigenous and fruit trees.

Learn More

Kisumu Youth Agri-Entrepreneurship

This program provides young aspiring farmers in the Kisumu region with training in modern, sustainable agricultural practices and the business skills needed to succeed.

Learn More

Coastal Fruit Orchard Development

To combat food insecurity in coastal regions, we are establishing drought-resistant fruit orchards that will provide both nutrition and income for local communities.

Learn More

Rift Valley Water Harvesting Initiative

In the arid regions of the Rift Valley, we are implementing innovative water harvesting and irrigation solutions to ensure the long-term success of our tree planting projects.

Learn More

Urban Farming in Nairobi Slums

We are promoting innovative urban farming techniques, such as vertical gardens and hydroponics, to provide fresh, nutritious produce to communities in Nairobi's informal settlements.

Learn More

Sustainable Coffee Production in Central Kenya

This project empowers youth in Central Kenya with the skills to cultivate high-quality, organic coffee, while also promoting fair trade practices to ensure they receive a fair price for their produce.

Learn More
